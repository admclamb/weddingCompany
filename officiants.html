Our Officiants

Dr.Bob Allen

Meet Doctor Bob Allen. He is my preferred Marriage Officiant. I have worked with many ministers and officiants over the years and hands down Dr. Bob Allen does the most personable heartfelt service I have had the privilege to witness. Bob is a ordained Christian Minister.


Like Tracey, he has such a love for uniting couples and this truly shows at each and every wedding we have preformed together. Most couples like to include a "Sand Ceremony" during a beach wedding. His version is so special and unique. he even has special things to do for blended families etc...


Dr. Bob Allen is a full-time wedding officiant and can cover any of the beaches in our coverage area. He is available for weddings from sunrise to sunset. He will perform heterosexual and gay couples ceremonies. Dr. Allen has officiated beach and resort weddings since 2009.


Dr. Allen writes ceremonies specifically for the couple and may include special words for children, family members, etc... He is knowledgeable about ancillary ceremonies such as the Sand, Wine Box, Unity Candle, Jump the Broom, Tree Planting, and Native American.

​

Bob's Sand Ceremony truly sets him apart. Each one is designed to be a memorable moment for each couple.


Once your wedding is booked and questionnaire is completed I will then forward this info to him and he will be in touch with you regarding your vows.



Sherri Crawford

Sherri has a natural talent for creating meaningful connections, whether in the community, within groups or between couples. Officiating weddings is a natural extension of that knack, and she considers it an honor to unite two people. Her background in communications helps her create a ceremony that reflects each couple’s unique bond and commitment.


From incorporating rituals or special passages, to simply finding the right words, her creativity can help guide each couple in creating a ceremony that’s truly special. Sherri appreciates all walks of life and performs unions and renewals regardless of gender, ethnicity or faith.


As an officiant, her style is warm and personable, and she’s a firm believer in being able to laugh at yourself.


She holds a B.A. in Communications from East Carolina University and is also an accomplished journalist, photographer, and broadcast and event announcer. When not performing wedding ceremonies, Sherri enjoys live music, travelling with her husband Matt, spontaneously dancing in the kitchen and taking a yoga class or two.
